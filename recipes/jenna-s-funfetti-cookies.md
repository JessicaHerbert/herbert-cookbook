## Ingredients

- ✨ 1 box funfetti cake mix
- 🌾 2 tablespoons flour
- 🥚 2 eggs
- 🫒 1/2 cup vegetable oil
- 🥄 1 teaspoon vanilla extract (plus a little extra)
- 🍬 3/4 cup powdered sugar

## Directions

1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees, line baking sheet with parchment paper – set aside
2. In a large bowl, stir together the cake mix and flour until well mixed. Stir in the eggs, oil and vanilla and mix until smooth
3. Using a tablespoon, form dough into balls, then roll in powdered sugar to coat
4. Place on prepared baking sheet, 2 inches apart.
5. Bake for 8-10 minutes until the edges are set and cookies begin to look cracked
6. Allow cookies to cool on baking sheets for 2 minutes before transferring them to wire racks to cool completely
