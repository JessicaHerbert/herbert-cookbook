## Ingredients

- 🍗 3-4 bone in pork chops (or boneless skinless chicken breast)
- 🥛 1 can campbell's cream of mushroom soup with garlic
- 🥛 Milk
- 🫙 3-4 tablespoons worcestershire sauce
- 🧄 1 teaspoon minced garlic
- 🧂 Creole seasoning
- 🫑 Salt and pepper
- 🫒 Olive or canola oil
- 🍗 ½ can chicken broth

## Directions

1. Instant Pot Garlic Pork Chops
2. Season pork chops generously with creole, salt and pepper.
3. Turn instant pot to saute with oil.
4. Brown each side of pork chops for 2 minutes
5. Add chicken broth to deglaze the bottom of the instant pot
6. While browning pork chops add soup, milk, worcestershire sauce, garlic, generous creole, salt and pepper in a separate bowl and mix well.
7. Add browned pork chops to deglazed instant pot with chicken broth. It’s ok to stack.
8. Add soup mixture on top
9. Place cover to instant pot on with valve set to seal.  Turn on manual (or high pressure) setting for 12 minutes
10. When instant pot beeps after 12 minutes at pressure, let sit for 15 before moving the valve to vent (watch out, it might spurt at  you)
11. Once pressure is released, remove lid and enjoy delicious pork chops and gravy.
