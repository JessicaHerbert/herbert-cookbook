## Ingredients

- 🥩 1 - 1½ lbs ground beef
- 🧅 1 medium onion, diced
- 🧄 4 garlic cloves, minced
- 🫘 2 cans (19oz) red kidney beans, drained and rinsed
- 🍅 3 tbsp tomato paste
- 🍅 1 can (28oz) crushed tomatoes
- 🌶️ 1-2 tbsp chili powder
- 🌿 1 tbsp ground cumin seed
- 🌿 1 tsp dried oregano
- 🫒 1 tbsp olive oil
- 🍯 Brown sugar to taste
- 🧂 Kosher salt and black pepper to taste
- 🥣 1 cup unsalted chicken stock
- 🐟 2 tbsp fish sauce
- 🥢 1 tbsp regular soy sauce
- 🍫 1 tsp unsweetened cocoa powder
- 🍋 1 tbsp apple cider vinegar (optional)
- 🌶️ https://www.pressurecookrecipes.com/instant-pot-chili/

## Directions

1. ⏲️ Heat Instant Pot on "Sauté More". Season ground beef with salt and pepper. Add olive oil and brown beef for 5-7 minutes. Remove beef juice at 5-minute mark and reserve. Continue browning until slightly crisped.
2. ⏲️ While beef browns, mix chicken stock, fish sauce, soy sauce, and cocoa powder in a measuring cup.
3. ⏲️ Add onions, garlic, cumin, chili powder, and oregano to pot. Sauté 5 minutes until fragrant.
4. ⏲️ Pour in ½ cup stock mixture. Scrub bottom of pot with wooden spoon to deglaze.
5. ⏲️ Add reserved beef juice, remaining stock mixture, and kidney beans. Mix well. Add tomato paste and crushed tomatoes on top. Do not mix.
6. ⏲️ Close lid, set to Sealing. Pressure Cook High 10 minutes, Natural Release. Open carefully.
7. ⏲️ If too runny, simmer on Sauté mode. Season with brown sugar, salt, and pepper. Add apple cider vinegar if desired.
8. ⏲️ Garnish and serve.
9. 📝 Adjust chili powder amount for kids - use 1 tbsp or less depending on spiciness and spicy-tolerance.
