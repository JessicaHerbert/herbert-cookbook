## Ingredients

- 🥓 1 ½ lbs Ham Hock or meaty ham bone
- ✨ 1 lb Navy Beans* (dry, or soaked 2 hours)
- 💧 6 cups Low Sodium Broth
- 🧅 1 large Yellow Onion, chopped
- ✨ 3 ribs Celery, chopped
- ✨ 3 Carrots, chopped
- 🧄 4 Garlic Cloves, finely minced
- ✨ 2 Bay Leaves
- 🌿 ¼ teaspoon dried Thyme
- 🌿 1 tablespoon dried parsley
- 🧂 ½ teaspoon Kosher Salt
- 🫑 ½ teaspoon Black Pepper

## Directions

1. Add all ingredients to the inner liner of the Instant Pot. Stir (Make sure the beans are fully submerged so you don't get any crunchy ones).
2. Pressure cook on high 45 minutes
3. When the cooking cycle ends, turn off the pot and let it sit undisturbed to naturally release pressure for 20 minutes.
4. Carefully release the remaining steam manually, using short bursts at first to make sure the soup won't spew out of the steam release knob. When all steam is out of the pot, and the pin in the lid drops, open the lid facing it away from you.
5. Serve hot with some nice bread or rolls.
