## Ingredients

- ✨ Pretzels
- ✨ Red & Green M&Ms
- ✨ Hershey hugs

## Directions

1. Preheat oven (or toaster oven) to 350 degrees.
2. Spread pretzels onto a baking sheet and top with hugs.
3. Bake pretzels for 2-4 minutes until chocolate begins to slightly melt.
4. Be sure to remove the pretzels before the chocolate melts too much, as you want the hugs to maintain their circular shape.
5. Quickly place one M&M in the middle of each white chocolate wafer.
6. Allow the pretzels to cool until the chocolate is completely hardened.
