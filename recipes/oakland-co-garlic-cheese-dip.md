## Ingredients

- 🥛 8oz Cream Cheese - brick
- 🥛 ½ cup Sour Cream
- 🫑 ¼ tsp white pepper
- 🧂 ¼ tsp salt
- 🧄 ¼ tsp garlic powder

## Directions

1. Soften cream cheese
2. Using a hand mixer or immersion blender mix cream cheese until fluffy
3. Add all other ingredients
4. Mix until well blended
5. Add to container and refrigerate at least 1 hour.
6. Use within 7 days
