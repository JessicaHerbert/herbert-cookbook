## Ingredients

- 🥩 Steak Bites:
- 🥩 1¾ lbs beef stew meat
- 🧂 1½ tsp salt, garlic powder, parsley and smoked paprika
- 🌾 1 tsp cumin
- 🍋 1 lime, juiced
- 🫒 2 tsp olive oil
- 🧈 ½ tbsp light butter
- 🧄 1 tsp minced garlic
- 🌶️ Chipotle Potatoes:
- 🥔 1½ lbs white potatoes, cubed
- 🌿 2 tsp Italian herbs
- 🧄 1½ tsp garlic powder, paprika and salt
- 🫒 2 tsp olive oil
- 🧄 1 tbsp minced garlic
- 🧅 ½ cup red onion, chopped
- 🌶️ 2 tbsp chipotle seasoning mix
- 🥛 1 cup light evaporated milk
- 🧀 ¼ cup cream cheese
- 🧀 ¼ cup grated parmesan cheese
- 🌿 Freshly chopped parsley

## Directions

1. Creamy Chipotle Steak & Potatoes
2. 🍳 Season steak with salt, garlic powder, parsley, smoked paprika, cumin, lime juice, olive oil, and minced garlic. Marinate at least 30 minutes.
3. 🍳 Bake or air fry potatoes at 400°F for 20-22 minutes.
4. 🍳 Cook steak in butter on medium-high heat for 2-3 minutes per side until browned. Don't overcrowd the pan.
5. 🍳 Cook minced garlic and onion for 5 minutes until soft. Add Italian herbs and chipotle seasoning, mix well.
6. 🍳 Add evaporated milk. Mix on low heat until smooth.
7. 🍳 Stir in cream cheese and parmesan. Add potatoes and toss to coat.
8. 🍳 Top with steak and garnish with parsley.
