## Ingredients

- 🧈 One stick of Butter
- 🧂 Two tsp. of Lawry’s Season Salt
- 🧄 One half tps. Garlic powder
- 🧅 One tsp. Onion powder
- 🌶️ One tsp. Chili powder
- 🫑 One half tsp. Red pepper
- 🫙 Six tsp. Worcestershire sauce
- ✨ (All the spices are rounded teaspoon full)
- ✨ 2 and 1/2 cups each
- 🍚 Rice Chex
- 🌽 Corn Chex
- 🌾 Wheat Chex
- ✨ Snyder’s Pretzel 🥨

## Directions

1. Heat oven to 250 degrees
2. Put cereal and pretzels in large container that has a lid
3. Using a two-cup glass measuring cup combine first five ingredients. Cover with a paper towel and heat two to three minutes in microwave.
4. Stir until well blended and pour over cereal. Cover and shake bowl. Tossing it around every which way to make sure it is well coated. You will be able to hear it when it seems evenly coated.
5. Put mix in shallow roasting pan. Bake in oven one hour stirring every 15 minutes.
