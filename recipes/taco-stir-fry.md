## Ingredients

- 🍗 1lb ground beef or turkey
- 🧂 2 packets taco seasoning
- ✨ 1 can black beans (rinsed and drained)
- ✨ 1.5 cups frozen mixed veggies
- 💧 ¼ cup water
- 🍚 3 cups brown rice
- ✨ Ortega Taco Sauce
- 🧀 Mexican 4 cheese
- ✨ Hint: 	Eat with Tostitos
- ✨ Salsa Verde Chips

## Directions

1. Brown ground beef / turkey
2. Add beans and veggies to pan and cover with taco seasoning - add water as needed to mix seasoning
3. Mix together and let set on low to come to temperature
4. While turkey mix is warming prepare rice following instructions on bag
5. Add rice to a bowl and cover with meat and veggie mixture.
6. Top with taco sauce and cheese
