## Ingredients

- 🥓 8 slices bacon
- 🥛 1 1/2 (8-oz.) blocks cream cheese, softened
- 🧀 1 c. shredded cheddar
- 🧄 1 tsp. garlic powder
- 🧂 1 tsp. paprika
- 🧂 Kosher salt
- 🫑 Freshly ground black pepper
- ✨ 1/3 c. freshly chopped chives
- ✨ 1/3 c. finely chopped pecans
- ✨ 18 pretzels sticks

## Directions

1. 1. In a large nonstick skillet, cook bacon until crispy, 8 minutes. Drain on a paper towel-lined plate, then finely chop. Set aside.
2. 2. Meanwhile, in a large bowl, stir together cream cheese, cheddar cheese, garlic powder, and paprika and season with salt and pepper. Use a cookie scoop to form mixture into 18 small balls and transfer to a parchment–lined baking sheet. Refrigerate until firm, 1 hour.
3. 3. In a shallow bowl, stir together bacon, chives, and pecans.
4. 4. Roll balls in bacon-chive-pecan mixture, insert a pretzel stick into each, and let come to room temperature 15 minutes before serving. (If not serving right away, loosely cover with plastic wrap and return to fridge.)
