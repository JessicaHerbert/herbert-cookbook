## Ingredients

- 🥩 2 lbs. Ground Beef
- 🧅 1 small diced onion (white or yellow)
- ✨ 1 4 oz can diced green chilies
- 🧂 2 packets of taco seasoning
- ✨ 1 jar salsa
- ✨ 2 cans enchilada sauce (mild)
- 🍅 1 15oz can of tomato sauce
- ✨ Soft taco shells
- 🧀 4 cups Mexican cheese blend
- 🫑 Salt and pepper

## Directions

1. Brown the ground beef and season with 2 tsp of salt – drain
2. Add onion and green chilies – cook for about 2 minutes
3. Add taco seasoning to coat meat followed by salsa and half can of tomato sauce plus water if needed. Let simmer for 30-60 minutes
4. Preheat oven to 350 degrees
5. In a separate bowl combine enchilada sauce with the other half can of tomato sauce
6. Coat baking dish with cooking spray
7. Drizzle a small amount of enchilada sauce mix to cover the bottom of dish
8. Assemble tortilla by filling with a large spoonful of meat mixture sprinkled with cheese and roll (do not fold ends)
9. Lay completed tortillas into baking dish side by side, push together as needed
10. Cover with remaining enchilada mixture and remaining cheese
11. Cook at 350 for 30 minutes
