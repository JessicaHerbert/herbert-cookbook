## Ingredients

### Chicken + Seasoning

- 🍗 ~10 oz boneless skinless chicken breast, diced
- 🧂 1 tsp salt
- 🌶️ ½ tsp chili powder
- 🧅 1 tsp onion powder
- 🧄 1 tsp garlic powder
- 🧆 1 tsp paprika
- 🧆 1 tsp cumin
- 🧂 ½ tsp black pepper

### Assembly

- 🌯 2 large tortillas (low carb/protein wraps)
- 🌯 2 small tortillas
- 🥛 ⅔ cup low fat Greek yogurt
- 🧀 ¾ cup low fat shredded cheese
- 🍅 1 tomato, diced
- 🌿 Shredded lettuce
- 🌮 10 tortilla chips (5 per wrap)

## Directions

1. Toss diced chicken with all seasonings.
2. Cook in a lightly oiled skillet over medium-high heat 5–7 min until done. Set aside.
3. Lay large tortilla flat. Spread Greek yogurt in center. Layer chicken, cheese, tomato, lettuce, and chips.
4. Place small tortilla on top to cap filling.
5. Fold large tortilla edges up and over, pleating to seal. Press down.
6. Cook seam-side down in an oiled pan over medium heat 2–3 min per side until golden. Slice and serve.
