## Ingredients

- 🥓 1 bag real bacon bits
- 🍗 ½ rotisserie chicken, shredded or cubed
- 🧄 1 jar garlic alfredo sauce
- 🫒 1 tablespoon olive oil
- 🫙 1 package ranch seasoning mix
- 🍝 8 ounces rotini pasta
- 🧀 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
- 🧀 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese

## Directions

1. Preheat oven to 375; lightly oil 9x9 baking dish or coat with nonstick spray
2. In a gallon Ziplock bag, add chicken, olive oil and ranch seasoning – shake to coat thoroughly
3. In a large pot of boiling salted water, cook pasta according to package instructions, drain well
4. Add pasta to the prepared baking dish and layer with chicken and alfredo sauce; sprinkle with cheeses and bacon.
5. Place into oven and bake until bubbly and heated through, about 15-20 minutes
6. Serve immediately
