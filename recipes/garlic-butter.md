## Ingredients

- 🧈 1/2 cup (8 tablespoons) unsalted butter
- 🧄 3 cloves garlic, crushed and finely minced
- 🫙 1/2 teaspoon Worcestershire sauce
- 🫑 1/4 teaspoon freshly ground white pepper, or black pepper
- 🧂 1/4 teaspoon salt, or to taste
- 🌿 1 tablespoon finely chopped fresh parsley

## Directions

1. Add name
2. Place 1/2 cup (8 tablespoons unsalted softened butter in a bowl and add 3 cloves garlic (finely minced), 1/2 teaspoon Worcestershire sauce,1/4 teaspoon freshly ground white or black pepper, 1/4 teaspoon salt, and 1 tablespoon finely chopped parsley. Mix with a fork to combine thoroughly.
3. Use a spatula to scrape the flavored butter onto a piece of plastic wrap. Using the wrap, shape the butter into a log. Pull the sides of the plastic wrap over to seal, twisting the ends to close tightly, or folding it like a small parcel.
4. Roll the wrapped butter back and forth to create an evenly shaped log. Place in the refrigerator or freezer to harden.
5. When ready to use, unwrap and slice the butter into 1/4-inch rounds. Place on top of hot, sizzling steaks just before serving. Serve and enjoy.
