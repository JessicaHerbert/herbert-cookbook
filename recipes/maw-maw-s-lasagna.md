## Ingredients

- 🥩 1-lb ground beef
- 🥓 1-lb Italian pork sausage (Bob Evans hot)
- 🧄 1 clove minced garlic
- 🌿 1 tablespoon parsley flakes
- 🧅 1 chopped onion
- 🫑 1 chopped green pepper
- 🧂 1.5 teaspoon salt
- 🍅 1 16oz can tomatoes
- 🍅 2 6oz cans tomato paste
- 🍝 1 box lasagna noodles
- 🧀 1-lb mozzarella cheese

## Directions

1. Brown meat slowly, sauté onion and pepper in fat.  Spoon off excess fat
2. Add garlic, parsley flakes, salt, tomatoes and tomato paste and let simmer until sauce is thick (about 1 hour), stirring occasionally.
3. Preheat Oven to 375 degrees
4. Cook noodles in boiling water until tender, drain and rinse in cold water
5. Place half noodles in a baking dish
6. Cover with sauce and mozzarella cheese
7. Repeat layers until dish is full – cover with cheese
8. Bake in oven for 30 minutes
9. Let stand for 15 minutes and cut into squares.
