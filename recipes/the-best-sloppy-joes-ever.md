## Ingredients

- 🥩 3 pounds ground beef
- 🧅 1 - white onion, diced
- 🫙 20-32oz ketchup
- 🌶️ 1 tsp Tabasco Sauce
- 🫙 Worcestershire Sauce
- 🍬 1/4-1/3 cup brown sugar

## Directions

1. Brown beef in large pot, drain
2. Add white onion to bottom of pan, sauté until almost translucent in half the pan (browned beef in other half)
3. Add full 20oz bottle of ketchup, mix well - more to taste. Make sure beef is well covered
4. Add brown sugar, mix well
5. Add Worcestershire sauce for color and to taste.
6. Add tabasco sauce
7. Allow to simmer for about 10-15 minutes adding additional tobacco or brown sugar to taste.
8. Serve hot or freeze
