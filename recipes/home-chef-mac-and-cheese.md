## Ingredients

- 🧈 2 tablespoons butter
- 🌾 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
- 🥛 2 cups heavy whipping cream
- 🫑 Salt & Pepper
- 🧀 1 cup chihuahua quesadilla cheese
- 🧀 1 cup freshly shredded cheddar cheese
- 🧀 ½ cup freshly shredded cheddar habanero cheese
- 🫙 2 tablespoons ranch seasoning
- 💧 ¼ cup water
- 🍝 Desired Pasta (cavatappi preferred)

## Directions

1. Spicy Ranch Mac & Cheese
2. Melt butter in a medium saucepan.
3. Once butter is melted, add 2 tablespoons of flour and stir with a whisk to prevent any lumps.
4. Add salt and pepper.
5. Cook and stir for 2 minutes.
6. Pour 1 cup of heavy whipping cream into the butter-flour mixture and continue stirring. It will start thickening in less than 1 minute.
7. Add the rest of the cream and stir. The sauce will then start reheating back up and be thicker within 3-5 minutes
8. Add ranch seasoning
9. Add cheese to melt and mix well
10. Add to cooked pasta
