## Ingredients

### Chicken

- 🍗 2 lbs chicken breast
- 🧂 Seasoning of choice, generous coat
- 🫒 Avocado oil for searing

### Street Corn

- 🌽 1 can corn
- 🧈 1 low-calorie butterball
- 🌮 Taco seasoning to taste

### Street Corn Sauce

- 🥛 ½ cup plain Greek yogurt
- 🫙 ⅓ cup light mayo
- 🌶️ 1 tsp Tajin
- 🌿 ½ tsp parsley flakes
- 🍋 Juice of 1 lime

## Directions

1. Season chicken breast generously — should be well coated on all sides.
2. Sear chicken in avocado oil over medium-high heat until cooked through with a good sear. Let rest.
3. In a separate pan, combine corn with butterball and taco seasoning. Mix and cook through.
4. Make street corn sauce: mix Greek yogurt, mayo, Tajin, parsley flakes, and lime juice.
5. Stir the cooked corn into the sauce.
6. Chop cooked chicken into small pieces.
7. Divide chicken into 4 meal prep containers. Top each with the street corn mixture.
