## Ingredients

### Chicken Marinade

- 🍗 ~14 oz chicken breast, diced into equal cubes
- 🧂 1 tsp salt + 1 tsp black pepper
- 🌿 1½ tbsp dried oregano
- 🥛 3 tbsp low fat Greek yoghurt
- 🧄 1 tsp minced garlic
- 🍋 2 tbsp lemon juice

### Tzatziki

- 🥛 ⅔ cup low fat Greek yoghurt
- 🧄 1 tsp minced garlic
- 🍋 Juice of ½ lemon
- 🥒 Cucumber, grated + drained
- 🌿 Fresh dill, finely cut
- 🧂 Salt + pepper

### 2-Ingredient Pita (makes 2)

- 🍞 1 cup all-purpose flour (protein flour preferred)
- 🥛 ½ cup low fat Greek yoghurt
- 🧂 1½ tsp baking powder + pinch of salt

### Fillings (per pita)

- 🧅 ¼ cup sliced red onion
- 🫒 2 tbsp black olives
- 🍅 4 cherry tomatoes
- 🧀 1 tbsp reduced fat feta
- 🌿 Fresh parsley

## Directions

1. Toss chicken with all marinade ingredients. Marinate at least 30 min (overnight is better).
2. Make tzatziki: grate + squeeze out cucumber. Mix with yoghurt, garlic, lemon, dill, salt + pepper. Refrigerate.
3. Mix pita dough: flour, yoghurt, baking powder, salt until soft. Divide into 2, roll out thin.
4. Cook pitas in a dry pan over medium-high heat 2–3 min per side until puffed + golden.
5. Cook marinated chicken in a hot pan or grill until cooked through and slightly charred.
6. Assemble: tzatziki on pita, top with chicken, onion, olives, tomatoes, feta, and parsley.
