## Ingredients

- ✨ All measurements are an estimate
- ✨ 1 - 24 oz jar Prego Traditional
- ✨ 1 - 23 oz jar Prego Marinara
- ✨ 2 - 22 oz jar Prego Homestyle Alfredo
- 🧀 1 - 8 oz Kroger Finely Shredded Italian Style Cheese
- 🧀 1 cup ricotta cheese
- 🧀 1 - ½ cup shredded gouda cheese
- ✨ 1 tablespoon dried minced onions
- 🫒 Olive Oil

## Directions

1. Cheesy Marinara
2. In a heated pot, add olive oil and minced onions.  Continuously move for about 30 seconds or until browned
3. In the same pan - add full jars of traditional, marinara and alfredo. Mix together
4. Once heated, ricotta cheese until well mixed
5. Add shredded cheeses, stir until mixed
6. Let simmer for 15-60 minutes
