## Ingredients

- 🍝 2 cups mini penne noodles
- 🧀 8 oz Velveeta cheese
- 🥛 ½ cup whole milk

## Directions

1. 1. Cook pasta according to directions, drain
2. 2. Cube cheese into small pieces and place into a microwave safe bowl. Add milk
3. 3. Microwave milk and cheese in 1 minute increments and stirring.  Continue until cheese is fully melted
4. 4. Add cooked pasta to bowl with cheese mixture and mix.
5. 5. Enjoy while still warm
