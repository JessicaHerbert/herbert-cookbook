## Ingredients

- 🫙 1 cup heavy-duty mayo chilled
- 🥛 ½ cup full-fat buttermilk
- 🧂 ⅛ tsp iodized salt
- 🧄 ¼ tsp each granulated onion, granulated garlic, cafe/table grind black pepper
- 🫑 ⅛ tsp cayenne pepper

## Directions

1. Outback copycat ranch
2. Whisk together the buttermilk and seasonings.
3. Add the mayo and mix until combined.
4. Marinate overnight before serving.
5. Notes:
6. Use within 5 days of production
