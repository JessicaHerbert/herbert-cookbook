## Ingredients

- 🫒 2 tablespoons olive oil
- 🧄 1.5 tablespoon garlic minced
- ✨ 1 cup onions diced
- ✨ 2 cup carrots diced
- ✨ 1 cup celery diced
- ✨ 4(ish) cups potatoes, peeled and chopped into 1 inch cubes
- 💧 4 cups vegetable broth
- 🥓 4 cups cooked ham chopped
- 🌿 1 teaspoon thyme
- 🧂 1/2 teaspoon salt (don’t forget to measure with your heart)
- 🫑 1/2 teaspoon pepper (don’t forget to measure with your heart)
- ✨ 1 can peas, drained
- 🥛 1.5 cups heavy cream (or milk of choice)
- 🌾 Optional to thicken : ¼ cup flour

## Directions

1. Set instant pot to sauté for 10 minutes. Heat olive oil.
2. Sauté garlic, onions, carrots, and celery until onions are translucent, about 10 minutes.
3. While veggies sauté,
4. Add all cooked vegetables + all remaining ingredients except milk and peas to the instant pot
5. Set instant pot to sealing, pressure cook for 8 minutes.
6. Once cooked, manually quick release the pressure in the instant pot
7. Stir in milk and peas
8. For thickening: add ¼ cup all purpose flour to a bowl. Scoop 1-2 cups of broth (it’s ok if a few veggie/ham pieces get in there too) into the bowl. Whisk together. Add flour mixture back into the soup and stir. Bring to a boil by setting it to Sauté for 2-3 minutes and your soup will naturally thicken.
