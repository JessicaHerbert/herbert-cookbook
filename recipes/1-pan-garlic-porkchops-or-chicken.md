## Ingredients

- 🍗 3-4 Bone in Pork chops or boneless skinless chicken breast
- 🥛 1 can of Campbell's cream of mushroom soup with garlic
- 🥛 Milk
- 🫙 2 Tablespoon Worcestershire Sauce
- 🧄 1 teaspoon minced garlic
- 🧂 Creole seasoning
- 🫑 Salt and pepper
- 🫒 1 Tablespoon olive or canola oil

## Directions

1. Cover meat with salt, pepper and creole seasoning.
2. In an oven safe frying pan (cast iron) add oil and brown meat on both sides.  Drain excess oil.
3. In a separate bowl combined soup, with 1 can full of milk, Worcestershire sauce, minced garlic and creole seasoning, salt and pepper to taste.  Mixing well
4. Add combined wet mixture to frying pan over already browned meat.
5. Cook on 375 for 45 minutes or 325 for 75 minutes.
