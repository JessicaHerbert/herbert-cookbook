## Ingredients

- 🥩 2 lbs. Beef Chuck Roast, cubed 1¼ inch
- 🫒 1-2 tablespoons olive oil
- 🧅 1 large onion, roughly sliced
- 🥕 3 medium carrots, chopped
- 🌿 3 ribs celery, chopped
- 🧄 6 garlic cloves, minced
- 🍃 2 bay leaves
- 🌿 1 teaspoon dried thyme
- 🍅 2 tablespoons tomato paste
- 🥣 5 cups unsalted chicken stock or beef stock
- 🧂 ¼ teaspoon fine salt
- 🥢 1 tablespoon regular soy sauce
- 🐟 1 tablespoon fish sauce
- 🌾 ¾ cup pearl barley
- 🧂 Kosher salt and ground black pepper to taste

## Directions

1. Heat Instant Pot on "Sauté More". Pat dry chuck roast, season with salt and pepper. Brown in oil for 8 minutes per side. Remove and set aside.
2. Add onions, sauté 4 minutes. Add carrots and celery, sauté 3 minutes. Set aside ⅓ of vegetables.
3. Add garlic, bay leaves, thyme, and tomato paste. Sauté 2 minutes.
4. Pour in 1 cup stock. Scrub bottom of pot with wooden spoon.
5. Turn off pot. Add salt, soy sauce, and fish sauce. Cut beef into 1¼ inch cubes. Add beef, remaining 4 cups stock, and barley.
6. Close lid, set to Sealing. Pressure Cook High 20 minutes, Natural Release 20 minutes. Open carefully.
7. Add set-aside vegetables. Press "Sauté" and simmer until vegetables are done.
8. Remove bay leaves. Season with salt. Garnish with parsley and serve.
