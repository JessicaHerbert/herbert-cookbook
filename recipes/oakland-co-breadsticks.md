## Ingredients

- ✨ 1 package frozen pizza dough
- ✨ Note: Pizza Buddy or Pepe’s from Walmart work best
- 🧈 ¼ Cup Butter - melted
- 🧄 Lawry's Kosher Classic Coarse Ground Garlic Salt

## Directions

1. Oakland Co Breadsticks (pair with garlic cheese dip)
2. Preheat oven to 400
3. Allow dough to thaw in refrigerator overnight
4. Spread dough into a rough square
5. Melt butter and spread across dough generously
6. Allow to sit for about 1 hour on tray in warm area - leaving on top of the warming oven in perfect
7. After dough has had time to rise, cut in half horizontally then into breadsticks
8. Sprinkle with garlic salt
9. Bake for 10-14 minutes until lightly browned
10. Remove from oven and enjoy immediately with Oakland Co Garlic Cheese Dip
