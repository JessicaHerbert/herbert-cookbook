## Ingredients

- 🧈 1/4 cup butter
- 🌾 1/4 cup flour
- 🍗 1 1/2 cups chicken broth
- 🥛 1/2 cup milk
- 🧂 1/4 tsp salt
- 🧄 1/2 tsp garlic powder
- 🧅 1/2 tsp onion powder
- 🌶️ 1/4 tsp paprika
- 🫙 1/4 tsp black pepper

## Directions

1. In a medium saucepan over medium-high heat, melt butter.
2. Once butter is melted, whisk in the flour until a paste forms.
3. Slowly pour in broth and milk, whisking the entire time, until smooth.
4. Add salt, garlic powder, onion powder, paprika, and black pepper and whisk to combine.
5. Reduce heat to medium and simmer, continuing to whisk constantly, for a few minutes until mixture thickens.
6. Use as desired, or incorporate into your favorite recipe in place of condensed canned "cream of" soups.
