## Ingredients

### Bowl

- 🍗 1½ cups shredded chicken breast
- 🧀 ¾ cup fat-free cottage cheese, blended smooth
- 🥛 2 tbsp fat-free Greek yogurt
- 🌶️ 2 tbsp buffalo sauce (Frank's)
- 🌿 ½ cup finely chopped celery + cucumber
- 🫙 1 tbsp chopped pickled jalapeños
- 🧂 Everything-but-the-bagel or ranch seasoning

### Crunch Topper (optional)

- 🌮 Crispy chickpeas or crushed protein chips

## Directions

1. Blend cottage cheese and Greek yogurt until completely smooth.
2. Stir in buffalo sauce and seasoning.
3. Fold in shredded chicken, celery, cucumber, and jalapeños.
4. Add crunch topper right before eating. Serve immediately.
