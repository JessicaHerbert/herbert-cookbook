## Ingredients

### Bowl

- 🍗 1 lb chicken breast or thighs, bite-sized chunks
- 🫒 1 tbsp olive oil
- 🧄 1 garlic clove, minced
- 🌿 1 tsp dried oregano
- 🧆 ½ tsp paprika
- 🍋 Juice of ½ lemon
- 🥔 2 cups sweet potatoes, peeled + cubed
- 🌾 2 cups cooked brown rice
- 🌿 3–4 cups chopped kale or mixed greens

### Creamy Garlic Sauce

- 🥛 ½ cup Greek yogurt (or dairy-free)
- 🫒 1 tbsp olive oil or avocado mayo
- 🧄 1 garlic clove, minced
- 🍋 1 tbsp lemon juice
- 🧂 1 tsp Dijon mustard
- 🧂 1–2 tbsp water to thin
- 🧂 Sea salt + pepper

## Directions

1. Preheat oven to 400°F. Toss sweet potato cubes with olive oil, salt + pepper. Roast 20–25 min until golden.
2. Season chicken with garlic, oregano, paprika, salt, pepper, and lemon juice.
3. Heat a skillet with 1 tbsp olive oil. Sear chicken 4–6 min per side until browned and cooked through.
4. Whisk together all sauce ingredients until smooth.
5. Build your bowl: rice + kale base, sweet potatoes one side, chicken the other. Drizzle with garlic sauce.
