## Ingredients

- 🍫 ⅓ cup Hershey’s cocoa
- 🧂 ½ tsp salt
- 🧈 ¼ cup butter
- 🍬 2 cups sugar
- 🥛 ½ cup milk
- ✨ 3 cups quick oats oatmeal
- 🧈 ½ cup creamy Jif peanut butter

## Directions

1. No bake cookies - OG Recipe
2. Combine cocoa, salt, butter and sugar into a sauce pan and bring to boil.  Let boil for exactly 1 minute.
3. Quickly remove from stove and add oats and peanut butter.
4. Spoon onto wax paper (move quickly) and allow to harden.
