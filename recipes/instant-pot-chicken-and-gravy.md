## Ingredients

- 🍗 2.5–3 lbs boneless skinless chicken breasts, each cut in half
- 🍲 2 cups low-sodium chicken broth
- 🫙 2 packets chicken gravy mix (0.87 oz each)
- 🫙 1/4 tsp black pepper
- 🧄 1/2 tsp garlic powder
- 🥛 2 cups [homemade cream of chicken soup](/recipes/homemade-cream-of-chicken-soup/)

## Directions

1. Place chicken breast pieces into the Instant Pot insert. Pour chicken broth into the pot.
2. Sprinkle gravy packets, black pepper, and garlic powder over the chicken — do not stir.
3. Close the lid and set the vent to sealed. Select 6 minutes at high pressure.
4. After cook time, allow a full natural pressure release. Once the valve drops, carefully remove the lid.
5. Remove chicken to a cutting board and shred, slice, or chop.
6. Whisk homemade cream of chicken soup into the gravy in the pot until fully incorporated and smooth.
7. Return chicken to the pot, stir to combine, and serve.
