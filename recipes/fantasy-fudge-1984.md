## Ingredients

- 🍬 6 cups Sugar
- 🧈 3 sticks butter
- 🥛 1 1/3 cups evaporated milk
- 🍫 2 12oz packages semi-sweet chocolate pieces
- ✨ 1 13oz jar of Kraft marshmallow crème
- 🥜 2 cups chopped nuts
- 🥄 2 teaspoons vanilla

## Directions

1. Combine sugar, butter and milk in a 5 quart pot; bring to full rolling boil, stirring constantly.  Boil 5 minutes over medium heat or until candy thermometer reaches 234, stirring constantly to prevent scorching.
2. Remove from heat, stir in chocolate pieces until melted. Add marshmallow crème, nuts and vanilla; beat until well blended.
3. Pour into two greased 13x9 inch pans.  Cool at room temperature; do not refrigerate.  Cut into squares.
4. NOTE: DO NOT substitute sweetened condensed milk for evaporated milk
